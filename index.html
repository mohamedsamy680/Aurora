About us

focus

Our focus has always been on using higher-quality ingredients and cooking techniques to make great food accessible to all people at reasonable prices. But our vision has evolved. While using a variety of fresh ingredients remains the foundation of our menu, we believe that "fresh is not enough, anymore.

Mission

Buffalo Burger, the Business of Good Food We aim to do a few things but do them exceptionally well. the goal was simple: to serve high quality, delicious food quickly with an experience that not only exceeded, but redefined the fast food experience and create "happy people = happy customer" concept.

Vision

The best quick service restaurant presenting gourmet burgers in a fine dine experience in the local, regiognal, and the international market. We aim to succeed in the four milestones of our business cores Customers, team members, franchisees, and shareholders.
